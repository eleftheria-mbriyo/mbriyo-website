The Gap

The best vertical AI founders are unfundable at the moment they need it most.

Too Early

No revenue. No traction. No comparable exit. The spreadsheet says no before the conversation starts.

Too Niche

The domain is unfamiliar. The moat is invisible to outsiders. The risk looks bigger than it is.

Too Technical

The product doesn't exist yet. Conviction required. Data unavailable.

We go in before the traction, into industries that have existed for decades but are now poised to be disrupted by AI.
That's where the best investments will be made and where founders need the most support, early.
How We Work

First check. Full stack.

We invest early, often alongside other investors in syndicated rounds, but capital is only one part of what we do. We identify high-potential teams with promising demos but in need of specialized support to scale. We step in not only with capital but also with an approach (architecture) that leverages venture partners' sweat equity.

  • Pre-seed cheque
  • First institutional investor
  • Founder-friendly terms, decisions in days
  • 1–4 week diagnostic sprint: cap table, GTM, product architecture
  • 12-month strategic guardianship
  • 2–3% equity, no capital from the fund
  • Shared 50/50 between fund and assigned Venture Partner
  • Combined investment + hands-on operational support
  • 2–4% services on top of capital
  • Maximum alignment, maximum support
  • For highest-conviction plays
Investment Venture Partners

Class C shareholders with long-term, carry-based alignment. Committed to the fund's portfolio for the long haul — skin in the game at the fund level.

Acceleration Venture Partners

Engaged per portfolio company, on a per-engagement basis. No capital required — brought in for their deep operational expertise to build alongside founders.

Investment Criteria

What we look for.

Workflow depth

AI-native products embedded in core workflows, not sitting alongside them

Data defensibility

Access to proprietary, domain-specific data that compounds over time

Revenue signal

Acute pain points, early buyer intent, or initial customer validation

Founder quality

Intelligence, grit, honesty, and the ability to learn from the market
